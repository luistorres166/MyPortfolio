Intro

Hello, my name is Luis! In 2008, after my first tour to Afghanistan, I began my journey to becoming a Full stack software engineer. Fast-forward to 2022 after my military career. I used my benefits with the VA to get into a program that helps Veterans develop tech skills and gain experience to get into the technology industry. I was Hand selected through a vetting process to work at Tekton. Check out my awesome work on GitHub.

I am passionate about web development and contributing to the virtual world we live in today. When Im out of the office, I enjoy the fantastic weather here in California from hiking to surfing on my bodyboard.

Work

I worked remotely for a start-up company called Tekton which is making an attempt to bring technology into the construction workspace and streamline the hiring process, by verifying the license and certifications of a worker on-site with a mobile-first application using a QR Code.

I developed this Fantasy Football League landing page using React.js, and styled-components.

Im currently working on a website for a client that owns and operates a tint shop in Texas. The next project, I will be working on will be a game using .net and C#
